ContactOur Product

Our bottle, which is 100% recyclable with its body made of 70% paper and a lid made of sugar cane;  dissolves within 3 months after its contact with the soil.

Thanks to the seeds in our packaging, our product is planting in nature.

All the products we use in our packaging are eco-friendly and do not harm the environment.



As LORAX Company, we are the invention of students who are sensitive to issues such as global warming and environmental pollution.

Our aim in this path, which we started in line with our sensitivity, is to bring nature consciousness to people with the product we produce and to leave a cleaner world for the future.

In line with this purpose, we are here to announce our service to more people.

Harmony with Nature


The greening bottle both prevents damage to nature and contributes to the ecosystem thanks to the seeds in its packaging.

Design

In addition to the aforementioned features, our greening bottle has been brought to the most ergonomic structure and the highest level of aesthetic beauty as a result of the long-term efforts of our teams.

pH 8.15


The water with a pH value of 8.15 in our bottle has many features such as holding
more oxygen, staying fresh longer and providing body balance.


Even the lid is not plastic.


The greening bottle, which was launched as a Lorax company, protects both human health and the world we live in from the harmful effects of plastic with the Zero Plastic label. The body part of our greening bottle is made of cardboard, and it meets the claim of zero plastic with its packaging structure that can dissolve in nature in a short time and even with the sugar cane raw material coming from nature itself instead of plastic in the lid part.


It preserves the water inside.


When plastic bottles are exposed to heat, the bottom usually becomes mossy. In this case, the algae that occurs can mix with the beverage in the bottle and harm human health, especially in summer.

Our product, on the other hand, is able to keep the beverage for a longer period of time without spoiling, thanks to its special packaging structure, and also eliminates the harms of its competitors to human health.


More eco-friendly.
More economical.


Our Greening Bottle is to raise awareness for all people.

Individuals who want to protect nature, animal lovers since no animal products are used in their production, and children and students have been determined as our target audience, since the outside of our bottles has a more aesthetic appearance than normal bottles and has cheaper prices in various colors.

Our Inspiration

Our biggest source of inspiration for this project is The Lorax is a 2012 animated film based on a children's book by Dr. Seuss. This movie takes place in a city where plastics have taken over nature. A city full of people convinced that there is nothing good out there, and that the earth and trees are filthy and rotten beings.


With this film, we have seen once again how much damage plastic actually causes to our lives and the environment we live in. Thus, we took the first step in our project with the ideas we got from this movie.
